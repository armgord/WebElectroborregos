Bringing Space Biology to Life

The data analyzed by the **Space Biology Knowledge Engine** comes from real missions and real experiments. This section provides the visual context, transforming cold data points into compelling stories of human ingenuity and biological adaptation in the cosmos.

Visual Assets Library

Explore a curated collection of videos, high-resolution imagery, and interactive models related to the scientific publications in our database.

  • Mission Footage: Videos showing crew members conducting biological experiments aboard the **ISS**, including plant growth and micro-organism studies.
  • Biological Models: 3D renderings and diagrams illustrating the effects of microgravity on cellular structures, muscle fibers, and bone loss studied in models like the **Bion-M 1 mice**.
  • Hardware & Facilities: Images of the specialized hardware and facilities used to conduct life science research in space, such as incubators and radiation monitors.
  • Orbital Views: Striking imagery and time-lapses of the Earth taken from the **ISS**, providing context to the environment where the data is collected.
