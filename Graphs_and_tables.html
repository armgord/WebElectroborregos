Visualizing the Microgravity Impact

The visualization core leverages the AI-processed publication data to offer dynamic views, making it easier to track trends across missions, organisms, and time periods. Use these visual tools to quickly gain insights into major biological domains:

  • Physiological Changes: Comparative charts showing the degree of **bone density loss** or **cardiovascular remodeling** across different animal models and mission durations.
  • Gene Expression Heatmaps: Interactive tables and heatmaps displaying patterns in **oxidative stress** or **DNA repair gene activation** following radiation exposure.
  • Cross-Species Analysis: Side-by-side tables comparing the effects of microgravity on diverse systems, from **plant root growth** (*Arabidopsis*) to **muscle atrophy** (mice).
